UNIVERSAL STORYLAB is a group of multi-format journalists with decades of experience that is ready to help you tell your story. We are at the cutting-edge, skilled in not only text, photo and video but also 360 photo and video and interactive presentations. Above all, we know how to tell stories. Let us tell yours.

We offer a number of services:

  • Beautiful text narratives
  • Journalistic-style photo essays
  • Documentary-style video.
  • 360 photo and video.
